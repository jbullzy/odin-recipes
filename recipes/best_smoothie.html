The Best Smoothie

About this dish

This is still the best smoothie recipe I've come up with. Low cal but it fills you with goodness. Careful with this one.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen blueberries or Berry Medley
  • Almond Milk
  • A scoop of your favorite protein powder
  • Cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons of Peanut Butter or Almond Butter

Directions

  1. Add your frozen blueberries and almond milk to the blender. The key is getting this ratio right - you want the almond milk to barely cover the frozen fruit.
  2. Add your Protein powder, a dash of cinnamon, and your peanut butter to the mix. Blend it up.
  3. Add almond milk as needed until you get the perfect consistency. It should be thick enough to scoop, but smooth enough to drink through a straw.
  4. Enjoy.
