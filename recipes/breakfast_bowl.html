Breakfast Bowl

About

This breakfast bowl is an old favorite of mine. Keto Friendly as well!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 cups Kale
  • 1/2 avocado
  • optional: Everything But the Bagel Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Put 2 cups of kale into pan on medium heat. Salt generously and add 1/4 cup of water before covering to sautee.
  2. Meanwhile, mix three eggs in a separate bowl. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Once the Kale has wilted, separate it into another bowl and butter the pan before adding the eggs.
  4. While eggs are scrambling, slice half an avocado the long way into nice, even slices.
  5. Before the eggs are completely finished, add your Kale back into the pan.
  6. Finally, serve the scramble in a bowl. Add your avocado and some Everything But the Bagel seasoning for a great morning.
